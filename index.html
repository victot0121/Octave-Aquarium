About Us

An Aquarium is a vivarium of any size having at least one transparent side in which Aquatic Plant and Animals and display are kept and display humans. Fish keepers use aquaria to keep fish, invertebrates, amphibians, aquatic reptiles, such as Turtles and aquatic Plant. The term Aquarium, coined by English naturalist philip Henry , combines the Latin root aqua, meaning 'Water', with the 'suffix arium, meaning place of relating to'
An Aquarium can be a small tank or large building with one more large tanks keeping an Aquarium is a popular hobby around the world. careful aquatics make sure their fish live in a environment similar to their natural habitats This means caring about water quality, lighting and food. large public aquarium's are often popular with tourists.
They show Animals and Fish keep in large tanks, and also often protect species that are close to extinction.
